How it started

From a home kitchen to your door

Kassy Bakes was never planned. It started the way the best things do — late nights, a warm kitchen, and an inexplicable need to bake something at midnight.

The first batch of brownies was shared with family. Then friends asked for cakes. Then friends of friends. Before we knew it, we had a little bakery on our hands.

Rico showed up first — uninvited, opinionated, and immediately obsessed with caramel. Sam arrived later, took one look at the brownie tray, sat down, and never left. They are not employees. They are definitely not the bosses. And yet, somehow, they are.
