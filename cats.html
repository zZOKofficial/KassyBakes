Chaos Department

Meet the Real Owners

🐾 · · · 🐾

They didn't ask to be mascots. They just showed up, demanded snacks, and somehow became the heart of everything Kassy Bakes is about.

🍮 Caramel Obsessed

Rico

Chief Taste Tester & Chaos Agent
🐾 · · ·

Rico arrived in the kitchen during a caramel sauce experiment and has never really left. He has a particular talent for appearing exactly when something smells good — and disappearing the moment there's work to do.

He is affectionate, loud, and will headbutt you until you share your butterscotch cake. Multiple attempts have been made to ban him from the kitchen counter. All have failed.

Fur Color Brown / Orange with black stripes
Eye Color Forest Green
Signature Move Batting ingredients off the counter
Weakness Caramel. Always caramel.

Rico's Flavor World

🍮 Caramel ☕ Coffee 🧈 Butterscotch 🍊 Warm Spices 🌰 Cinnamon
"Rico once knocked an entire caramel cake off the counter, caught a piece mid-air, and looked us dead in the eyes while eating it. We couldn't even be mad."
— Kassy, Founder
🐾🐾🐾

Sam

Chief Inspector & Midnight Chocolate Critic
🐾 · · ·

Sam is harder to read. He appears silently, sits somewhere high up, and watches every step of the baking process with an expression that could mean approval or total contempt. Nobody is ever really sure.

He is drawn exclusively to dark chocolate and midnight hours. He has been caught, on more than one occasion, sitting next to a brownie tray at 2am with absolutely no remorse.

Fur Color Pure Black — sleek and velvety
Eye Color Gleaming Yellow
Signature Move Silent stare of judgment
Weakness Dark chocolate. Won't admit it.

Sam's Flavor World

🍫 Dark Chocolate 🍪 Brownies 🌙 Midnight Vibes ✨ Premium Ganache 🍒 Bittersweet
"Sam once sat on the brownie box for 40 minutes. We don't know if he was guarding it or just warming it up. We called it quality control and moved on."
— Kassy, Founder
