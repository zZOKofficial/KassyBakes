The real owners

They didn’t apply for the job. They didn’t interview. They just sat on the cake boxes until everyone accepted the truth — Rico and Sam run this place.

Cat 01

Rico

The warm, playful one 🦁

Lion-maned and caramel-obsessed, Rico is pure warm chaos. He’s the first to investigate any new smell from the oven, the loudest advocate for “just one more taste test,” and the reason the butterscotch sauce lives on a high shelf. If your cake feels like a hug, that’s Rico’s influence.

VibeEnergetic, affectionate, food-motivated, gloriously chaotic

Cat 02

Sam

The mysterious, elegant one 🌙

Sleek, black, and impossibly composed, Sam is the bakery’s silent quality control. He observes from the highest shelf, says nothing, and approves of almost nothing — which is exactly why his approval means everything. He has a documented weakness for chocolate brownies at 4am, though he’ll deny it under oath.

VibeObservant, dramatic, elegant, silently chaotic
RepresentsChocolate, dark flavours, premium cakes, midnight café energy
